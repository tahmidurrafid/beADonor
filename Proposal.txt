BeADonor
Introduction:
This system will provide donation service. There will exist collaborations between both type of people
who will donate and who needs donations. This will run as charitable and non profit organization. On our 
webapp, moderators will work as bridge between the donors and the people who seek help.
Our system will provide the opportunity for all sorts of donations. For example, there will be
    blood donation, 
    old and unused clothes(specially in winter Season) will be collected and distrubuted among the needy people
    People can ask for financial support for critical medical issue. Our moderator will verify their documents
        and then the post will be visible to donors. 
    Users will submit details of their unused study material(books/bags/calculator ...), gadgets(phone/laptop)
        and other tools. Our moderator will review and pick up goods from their location or ask them
        send through courier(depending upon the distance)
